About Me

I was born on February 15, 2000, and my life has been a journey of growth, resilience, and purpose. From my early days in the DMV area to working at Microsoft in NYC, every step has shaped who I am. I’m passionate about mental health advocacy, international development, and creating impact.

My Mission

To live with integrity and alignment—trusting my intuition, honoring my inner wisdom, and expressing myself fully. I seek to move through life with presence and courage, grounded in a deep understanding of who I am and committed to showing up as that person, always.

What I Care About

I care deeply about family, wellness, global justice, and building community. Whether it's running a marathon, supporting my brother, or volunteering with BRC and NAMI-NYC, I try to live with purpose.
